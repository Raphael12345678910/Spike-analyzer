SpikeCheck

Upload a side-view volleyball hitting clip and get more useful form feedback.

How this works:
You can use SpikeCheck with only a video for basic form feedback.

If you also enter your standing reach and net height, and then click the top of the net and the floor directly below it on the video, SpikeCheck can also estimate:
• contact reach
• gain above standing reach
• rough vertical/contact height estimate

To improve accuracy:
• use a side-view clip
• keep the full body visible
• make sure the top of the net and the floor under it are visible
• use a stable camera and decent lighting
• keep only one hitter in frame if possible
Optional net calibration
If you entered a net height, you can click the button below and then click:
1. the top of the net
2. the floor directly below it
Calibration not set.
